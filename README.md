# Sendly

> **Dispatch funds by nickname on any social network**

**Sendly** makes it easy to unlock stablecoins for any person, even if they don't have a crypto wallet yet.

---

- **Live Site:** [sendly-app.vercel.app](https://sendly-app.vercel.app)

## Overview

Sendly was born out of a desire to make crypto accessible to everyone, thereby building that very bridge between web2 and web3 users.

The main idea is to send tokens via the username of the most popular social networks. 

---

## Key Features

### Easy Token Dispatch
Send stablecoins to anyone using their social media username - no wallet required for recipients!

![Sendly Main Interface](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9eeaee4e-0a0e-499f-bdd1-2cdac6f0004a)

### Spend the card on whatever you want
Recipients can activate the card in a couple of clicks and receive funds to their wallet after checking the status and ownership of the token. 


![Card Activation](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a8cc80e3-9a72-427d-b9b5-edc84547b5f1)

### Management Tools
- **Contact Manager** - Keep track of your contacts
- **History & Analytics** - View all operations with detailed analytics
- **Filters & Exports** - Advanced filtering and data export capabilities
- **Voice Agent Integration** - Automated limit checks and voice commands
- **Expense Planner** - Plan and manage your expenses

![Management Dashboard](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/25001048-4ed4-4dea-9772-542e16485169)

### AI Voice Agent

The agent is based on the orchestration of **Langraph** and **Telegram bot**. You can:
- Send funds using voice commands
- Check your balance
- Plan expenses through the planner

![Voice Agent](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6b824984-1012-4f19-84fe-2bc4544cffea)
